The AI Bootcamp is a 12-week intensive program designed to train future AI engineers.
The schedule is as follows:
Week 1-3: Foundations of AI and Engineering
Week 4-6: Advanced LLM concepts
Week 7-9: Project Phase 1
Week 10-12: Final Project and Demo Day.

Office hours are held every Tuesday and Thursday at 4 PM EST.
The program is remote-first.
Mentors are available via Discord for questions.
